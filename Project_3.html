Crêpes (Crepes)

Who doesn't love crepes? Everyone like it but nobody knows how to make one, so have a seat, and let me teach you how to make one

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp of sugar
  • 2 tbsp of melted butter
  • 1 tsp of vanila
  • A pinch of salt

Bonus ingredients :

  • Matcha powder
  • Chocolate
  • Treats
  • Fruits

Cream filling

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2-4 tbsp of sugar
  • 1 tsp of vanila

Instructions

  1. Mix everything (or blend it): Milk + eggs first. Then flour + sugar + salt. Then butter + vanilla
  2. Let it rest (20-30 minutes) in the fridge
  3. Heat a non-stick pan (medium-low heat). Pour a small amount of batter and swirl thinly. Cook ~30 sec each side
  4. Make the cream by mixing heavy cream with sugar and vanila
  5. Assemble it by place a crepe and a thin layer of cream
  6. Then, let it cool in the fridge for atleast 2.5 hours
